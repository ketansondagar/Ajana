    CORN STARCH INDUSTRIAL GRADE

    In the textile industry, Corn Starch Powder serves various purposes to enhance fabric quality. It is employed to improve surface texture, enhance dry strength, and aid in wet end sizing. Additionally, it can be used to add weight to fabrics. Sahyadri Starch & Industries Pvt. Ltd (SSIL) produces Corn Starch Powder (MSP) specifically designed for the textile sector. Our MSP helps keep yarn straightened, enhancing its ability to withstand the stress of weaving. Our textile- grade Corn starch products are well-regarded by all kinds of wrap sizing processors, including those with high-standard automation, as they maintain weaving efficiency while offering cost competitiveness. A notable advantage of CORN Starch Powder is its complete biodegradability, making it an environmentally friendly choice for textile applications.
